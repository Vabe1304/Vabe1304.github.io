About Me

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer at Loka, developing AI systems that accelerate drug discovery, helping develop drugs quicker and at lower costs to democratize access. I also co-design executive innovation programs at GenH, facilitating dialogue between senior pharmaceutical leaders and young professionals.

As a member of the Global Shapers Community, I lead digital upskilling initiatives for high school students from underrepresented communities.

This portfolio brings together my work at the intersection of technical AI development and healthcare leadership transformation.
